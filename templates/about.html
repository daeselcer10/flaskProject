Frequently Asked Questions about Bookshelf


Who are you?

My name is Dae. Hi!

Are you a coder?

No. I learned (some) Python so I could make this game. I had help with some JavaScript bits (see the credits below). I know my code...isn't the most elegant. If you want to yell at me about it, click here.

Why did you make this?

I like to have fun projects. Also, I made a paper version of it for a friend, and then wondered what a web-based version would be like.

Is this like Wordle? Do you play Wordle?

I guess you could say that this game was inspired by Wordle, though there are some significant differences. Wordle is all done in JavaScript; Bookshelf is actually powered by Python. Also, in Bookshelf the answer could be any length, so the answer-input and checking mechanisms are different from Wordle's. Also, in Bookshelf, you get clues. And guess who had to write all those clues? Me. It was fun though!

And yes, I do play Wordle every day. Other daily games include Worldle, Globle, Flagle, Capitle, and Framed.

Gee, I sure do love this game. What can I do to show my appreciation?

Please consider sending me a tip! I am currently a doctoral student who also works full time, so I very much need the money. Thank you!

Why doesn't this game let me keep track of my streak? Wordle lets me keep track of my streak! Why do you hate fun?

I would really love to add more features to Bookshelf! I'm working on it, I swear. If you have an idea for me, send me an email, or better yet, buy me a coffee.
